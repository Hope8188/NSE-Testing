range of courses and qualiﬁcations designed for professionals in the 
ﬁnancial services industry. The CISI is a global professional body that 
sets standards of professional excellence and integrity for individuals 
working in securities, investment, wealth, and ﬁnancial planning.

Financial Literacy
For High Schools
The ﬁnancial literacy for High school students program equips young 
investors with essential knowledge for a secure ﬁnancial future. The 
program empowers students to navigate the complex 
world of ﬁnance. We provide valuable resources to kickstart their 
ﬁnancial journey and delve into speciﬁc investment vehicles like 
money market funds, bonds, equities, real estate investment trusts, 
and exchange-traded funds. The program concludes with an 
enriching exchange visit and a graduation ceremony, celebrating their 
newfound ﬁnancial acumen.

Challenge 2026

challenge encourages strategic decision-making and understanding 
market dynamics while fostering ﬁnancial literacy in a safe,
