## The 5 Compliance Gaps Identified

### ❌ Gap 1: No Independent Directors
**Finding:** 0 out of 9 directors identified as independent  
**SASRA Requirement:** Minimum 1/3 of board must be independent non-executive directors  
**Page Reference:** Pages 6, 10-14 of annual report  

### ❌ Gap 2: Independence Requirement Not Disclosed
**Finding:** No disclosure of compliance with 1/3 independence rule  
**Risk:** Regulatory non-compliance  

### ❌ Gap 3: Audit Committee Independence Unverified
**Finding:** Audit & Risk Committee chair independence not stated  
**SASRA Requirement:** Majority of audit committee members must be independent  
**Page Reference:** Pages 67-70  

### ❌ Gap 4: Governance Committee Missing
**Finding:** No governance committee disclosed  
**SASRA Requirement:** Required under GG/2/2023 Section 11  

### ❌ Gap 5: CFO Not Disclosed
**Finding:** Chief Financial Officer position not explicitly identified  
**Risk:** Key management disclosure gap
